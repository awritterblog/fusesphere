The Story of the Bean Pie

Before diving into what makes Shaba’s Bean Pies special, it’s worth appreciating the history behind this beloved dessert. The bean pie is a cornerstone of African American culinary heritage. Made from navy beans, sugar, butter, milk, and aromatic spices, it’s a creation born of resourcefulness and love.

For generations, families have gathered around the table to share bean pies during holidays, community events, and Sunday dinners. What started as a humble, home-baked dish has now become a symbol of culture, pride, and connection.

Today, Shaba’s Bean Pies continues that legacy with a mission to share this classic dessert with the world — while keeping its authenticity intact. Every pie is baked using traditional methods, ensuring that same comforting flavor that has made bean pies a soul food essential for decades.
